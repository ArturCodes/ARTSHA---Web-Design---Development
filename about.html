about me

How My Journey Began

Hi there, my name is Art. I’m the owner and the sole developer for ARTSHA. This business idea was not an overnight thing, and in fact it took several years to get here. After graduation I spent a lot of time exploring many different career options, trying to find my true calling.

I’ve learned a lot about myself during that time, my strengths and weakness, what I liked and disliked. I don’t exactly recall how my initial interest in coding started, but I do remember casually exploring it and teaching myself in my spare time.

When finally this exciting business idea came about, something sparked in me. I knew right way that this is what I wanted to do, and so I decided to fully commit to its creation. I studied marketing, web design, all the proper coding techniques, and everything in between.

I learned how Google ranks websites, and what is required for a website to succeed. And as I acquired new knowledge, my self-confidence and conviction grew, enabling me to turn my this idea and this passion into a reality.

What Sets Artsha Apart

We treat your websites like big business treat theirs. When we work with you, we will go above ands beyond to make sure you’re satisfied with your website and we will ensure that it performs at its best and just as importantly that it delivers the results that matter.

Hand Coded Websites

Unlike many of our competitors, we don’t rely on WordPress, frameworks, or pages builders to make websites. Instead, we write our code line by line, giving us full control over the design and performance of the website.

Professionally built websites will out-perform the vast majority of WordPress sites and drag and drop page builders, simply because they are not bloated with messy and unnecessary code or slowed down by 3rd party plugins.

Affordability

A professionally built website can transform and elevate an entire business. Understandably not many small business can afford to spend thousands of dollars from the get go.

What we offer is a low cost & low risk subscription based services that is aimed to help small business grow and succeed without breaking their bank.

Customer Service

What makes us different is that we work with one client at a time. It gives us the flexibility and that time to ensure that the final website is top quality and that you are completely satisfied with it.

You will be essentially getting a personal web developer, who is going to be with you every step of the development process, and long after when everything is done, supporting you with anything you many need.
