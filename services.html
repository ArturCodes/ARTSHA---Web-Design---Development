Prioritizing Mobile Experience

In 2015 about 30% of all internet traffic was accessed through mobile devices, in 2018 it reached 50%, and today that number is over 60%. When Google noticed this trend they developed a set of guidelines known as "mobile-first indexing."

What that means is that google, now prioritizes websites that are mobile friendly first when determining a website search ranking. If the website is not mobile-friendly google can even penalize it.

We make sure your website doesn’t face that problem, by not only making your website look and functions great on mobile devices, but also across all screen sizes and devices.
